**Location:** Hong Kong

**Year opened:** 1980 (opened as The Regent Hong Kong; rebranded in 2001 and again in 2023)

Intercontinental Hong Kong is known for its harborfront skyline address and status as one of Kowloon's defining luxury hotel identities across multiple brand eras.
