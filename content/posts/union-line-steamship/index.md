**Location:** Dunedin, New Zealand

**Year opened:** 1875 (operations wound down by 2000)

Union Line Steamship is known for its reach across trans-Tasman and Pacific routes as the "Southern Octopus," a near-monopoly carrier that shaped regional passenger and freight movement for more than a century.
