**Location:** Chicago, Illinois and San Francisco, California, USA

**Year opened:** 1936 (discontinued in 1972)

Streamliner City of San Francisco Train is known for its flagship streamliner service on the Overland Route, cutting premium transcontinental times and defining pre-jet rail glamour.
