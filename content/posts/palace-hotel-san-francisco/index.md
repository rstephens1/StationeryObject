**Location:** San Francisco, California, USA

**Year opened:** 1875 (rebuilt and reopened in 1909 after the 1906 earthquake and fire)

Palace Hotel San Francisco is known for its role as a civic-stage hotel where business, politics, and West Coast society repeatedly crossed paths.
